Brenda J. Shores is a lifelong Kansas City resident who has helped people buy and sell homes throughout Kansas and Missouri since 1997 — steady guidance, clear communication, and genuine care in every transaction.

She holds CRS, GRI, ABR, and CFSP designations, trains continuously through Buffini and Company, and has earned RE/MAX Hall of Fame, Chairman's Club, and Platinum Club recognition along the way. Ninety-eight percent of her business comes from repeat and referral relationships.
